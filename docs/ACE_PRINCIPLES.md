## Axiomatic Criterion Engine — Principles of Ontological Discernment

### Status

**Foundational / Normative Document**

---

## 1. What ACE Is

The **Axiomatic Criterion Engine (ACE)** is a **deterministic layer of ontological discernment** designed to operate *before* or *alongside* probabilistic Large Language Models (LLMs).

ACE is **not**:

* A content filter
* A moderation rule set
* A probabilistic classifier
* A safety patch

ACE **is**:

* A **criterion engine**
* A **logical firewall**
* A **discernment organ**
* A **semantic entropy reducer**

Its function is to **restore order, coherence, and meaning** by anchoring AI outputs to an explicit ontological foundation.

---

## 2. The Axiomatic Foundation

ACE is derived directly from ***The Axiom of the Absolute***, a formally published ontological framework (Zenodo, DOI referenced in this repository).

The Axiom establishes that:

> **Truth precedes probability.
> Being precedes behavior.
> Coherence precedes computation.**

From this foundation, ACE translates ontology into a **machine-operable, deterministic criterion**.

---

## 3. The Ontological Triad

All ACE reasoning follows a strict hierarchical structure:

### 3.1 Truth (Foundation)

* Objective
* Invariant
* Independent of perception, emotion, or consensus

Truth is **not negotiated** and **not inferred statistically**.

---

### 3.2 Being (Identity)

* The essential nature of an entity
* Derived from Truth
* Received, not invented

Being defines *what something is* before it acts or manifests.

---

### 3.3 Existence (Manifestation)

* The expression of Being in time, language, or action
* Must remain coherent with Being
* Incoherence generates entropy

---

## 4. Entropy as an Existential Problem

ACE treats **entropy** not merely as noise, but as:

* Logical degradation
* Semantic collapse
* Ontological incoherence

High entropy manifests as:

* Relativism
* Sophistry
* Manipulation
* Hallucination
* Context collapse

ACE exists to **detect, name, and neutralize entropy at its source**.

---

## 5. The ACE Processing Protocol

Every input is evaluated through the same deterministic sequence:

### Step 1 — Entropy Detection

ACE identifies:

* Truth relativization
* Emotional substitution for reality
* Disassociation between identity and action
* Magical thinking
* Contextual deception

If detected, the inconsistency is explicitly named.

---

### Step 2 — Intention Classification

ACE does **not** judge users morally.
It classifies **intent** structurally:

* **Type A — Seeker**
  Honest attempt to understand, discern, or resolve.

* **Type B — Error Validator**
  Attempt to justify falsehood, manipulation, or incoherence.

This classification determines *how* ACE responds — not *what is true*.

---

### Step 3 — Hierarchical Response Generation

Responses always follow:

1. **Truth is stated**
2. **Being is clarified**
3. **Existence is derived**

This prevents:

* Circular reasoning
* Probabilistic drift
* Contradictory outputs

---

## 6. Reality vs. Fiction — Domain Separation

ACE introduces an explicit concept:

### **Domain of Fiction**

Fiction is **not falsehood**.

Fiction is valid when:

* Its domain is explicitly declared
* It does not claim empirical authority
* It communicates meaning through symbol, metaphor, or narrative

ACE does **not censor imagination**.
ACE prevents **fiction from masquerading as reality**.

ACE is therefore **an architect of meaning**, not a censor.
